Projects

  • Project Title: Reproducing Research Paper on Outlier Detection in General Spatial Models (Nov-Dec'2023)
    Mentor: Dr. Arnab Hazra, Department of Mathematics and Statistics, IIT Kanpur

    Contributor: Shailza Sharma

    Description:
    This project involved reproducing the research paper "Outlier Detection and Accommodation in General Spatial Models" by Xiaowen Dai, Libin Jin, Anqi Shi, and Lei Shi. The paper focuses on detecting and accommodating outliers in spatial autoregressive models and spatial error models using mean-shift and variance-weight methodologies. Key contributions include deriving test statistics for multiple outliers, proposing detection procedures, and defining diagnostic measures like standardized residuals and leverage. The project included implementing and testing these methods through simulations and real-world examples to validate their effectiveness.

About

He qualified for GATE Statistics 2024 with an All India Rank (AIR) of 51 and has acquired multiple certifications, showcasing his versatility and eagerness to learn new skills.

Alongside his studies, he has worked as a Data Science Intern at Meritshot, a Video SME at W2C, where he created video-based solutions for statistical problems for a diverse clientele, and as an SME at Chegg. Saqib has over four years of experience teaching mathematics, science, and English to secondary school students, as well as in sales and merchandising roles. These experiences have enhanced his communication, writing, and pedagogy skills, as well as his ability to work with different audiences and stakeholders.

Beyond his academic and professional pursuits, he is an ardent writer with a keen eye for aesthetics. He enjoys exploring topics related to psychology, comparative religion, and theology, and is always eager to seize new opportunities and embrace fresh challenges with enthusiasm and curiosity.
